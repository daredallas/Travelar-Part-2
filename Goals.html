Goals

Goals for Stakeholders

App developers: Money, success of the app, adoption by travel agencies, data, incentivising businesses to partner
Travel agencies: Making their customers' experience easier and more memorable, increasing customer satisfaction
Attractions and businesses: Feedback from travelers and more potential customers
Local governments: Money from tourism, feedback
Travel bloggers: Creating higher quality content, with more ease


Goals for Customers

Saving time searching for information
Saving time searching for locations
Saving money by showing prices and by showing which location's aren't worth visiting
Discovering new information
Discovering new locations
Satisfaction with the product's features
